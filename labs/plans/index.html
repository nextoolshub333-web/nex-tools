FAQ

Frequently asked.

How fast do I receive content?
First articles land within seven business days of brief approval. After that, content ships on a weekly cadence throughout the month.
Who actually writes the articles?
A human editorial team using a proprietary research and drafting pipeline. Every piece is reviewed for voice, facts, and brand fit before it reaches you.
What are the custom interactive tools?
Calculators, quizzes, configurators, and budget estimators built specifically for your industry. Example: a Kitchen Investment Calculator for a luxury kitchen studio. Each tool is a standalone page that captures leads and boosts time-on-site.
Can I change plan or cancel?
Yes. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel any time from your account. Changes take effect the next billing cycle. You keep everything already published.
Do I need to brief every article?
No. We build a quarterly content calendar from your brand brief and competitor research, then you approve the calendar once. Individual articles do not need sign-off unless you want it.
What happens if I hate an article?
One free rewrite per article, no questions. If a piece still is not right after that, it does not count toward your monthly quota. You never pay for work you cannot use.
Can I use the content anywhere?
Yes. Full commercial ownership transfers to you on publication. Use it on social, in emails, in print, in ads. It is yours.
Which plan should I choose?
Starter if you are validating the channel. Growth if you want both content volume and lead-generation tools. Flagship if you want to dominate a category with backlinks and social amplification layered on top.
