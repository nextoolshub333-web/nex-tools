What is Rose Quartz?

Rose Quartz has been the universal stone of love for at least five thousand years. Egyptian and Roman women used Rose Quartz face rollers believing they preserved youth and beauty. Ancient Greek mythology says Eros brought Rose Quartz to Earth to inspire love. Rose Quartz was found in Sumerian tombs dating to 7000 BCE. Across every culture that has worked with stones, the soft pink stone has been the stone of the heart - of self-love, partner love, family love, and the broader universal love that includes all three.

Rose Quartz forms in massive veins rather than individual crystals - finding a perfectly terminated Rose Quartz point is extremely rare. Most Rose Quartz is cut from large rough chunks into tumbles, hearts, spheres and wands. Brazil and Madagascar are the primary sources.

How to Cleanse Rose Quartz

Cleansing clears accumulated energetic debris from Rose Quartz. Cleanse after any heavy emotional use, after difficult conversations, after illness, and at minimum once per month as maintenance. Three primary methods:

WATER CLEANSING

Safe for running water cleansing; 30 to 60 seconds under cool water is ideal. Rose Quartz is also one of the few crystals that can be safely soaked in saltwater (briefly, 5 to 10 minutes) for deeper cleansing.

SMOKE CLEANSING

Sage, rose petal smoke, or palo santo all cleanse Rose Quartz beautifully. Rose petal smoke (burning dried organic rose petals briefly) is especially resonant.

MOONLIGHT CLEANSING

Full moon overnight. Rose Quartz resonates with lunar energy, especially the full moon's completion frequency - ideal for cleansing and recharging.

How to Charge Rose Quartz

Charging is different from cleansing. Cleansing clears old energy; charging adds new energy or focus. Charge Rose Quartz after cleansing, when starting a new intention, or when you feel the stone has gone quiet. Three primary charging methods:

SUNLIGHT

AVOID prolonged sunlight. Rose Quartz's pink color fades with extended UV exposure. Limit to 20 to 30 minutes in morning sun only.

INTENTION SETTING

Hold Rose Quartz to the heart center, close your eyes, and speak the specific love intention: self-love, attracting a partner, healing a family relationship, forgiving yourself. The stone will carry that specific vibration until recharged.

MOONLIGHT

Full moon night is the primary charging period. For partner-love work, charge during the waxing moon. For self-love work, charge during the full moon. For release work, charge during the waning moon.

How do I know if Rose Quartz is real or fake?

Rose Quartz authenticity can be verified by: hardness testing (should match Mohs 7 (Mohs scale)), visual inclusions (natural Rose Quartz typically has internal variation, not perfect uniformity), weight (genuine crystal has denser feel than glass or plastic), and purchase source (reputable crystal shops and mineral galleries provide provenance). When in doubt, consult a gemologist or use the Gemological Institute of America (GIA) standards for reference.
