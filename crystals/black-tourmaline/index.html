What is Black Tourmaline?

Black Tourmaline has been worn and placed at doorways for psychic protection for thousands of years. Ancient magicians used it as a repellent against negative entities and harmful intentions. Modern crystal work honors Black Tourmaline as the most reliable energetic shield - it does not block love or positive energy, but it deflects psychic attack, energetic vampirism, environmental electromagnetic frequencies, and lingering lower vibrations. Its practical applications have made it one of the most purchased crystals in the past decade as EMF awareness has grown.

Black Tourmaline (schorl variety) forms in granite pegmatites through boron-rich hydrothermal activity. It crystallizes in striated (ridged) columns, often attached to Clear Quartz and Mica. Brazil, Afghanistan, Madagascar and the American Southwest (especially California and Maine) are major sources.

Black Tourmaline authenticity can be verified by: hardness testing (should match Mohs 7 to 7.5 (Mohs scale)), visual inclusions (natural Black Tourmaline typically has internal variation, not perfect uniformity), weight (genuine crystal has denser feel than glass or plastic), and purchase source (reputable crystal shops and mineral galleries provide provenance).
