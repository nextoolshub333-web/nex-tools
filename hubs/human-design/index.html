What Human Design Is

Human Design is described by its originator as "the science of differentiation" - a framework for understanding how each person's energetic design differs from every other person's. Unlike astrology's continuous spectrum, HD produces a specific chart with discrete categorical outputs: you are one of 5 types, one of 7 authorities, one of 12 profiles, with one of 5 Definition configurations. This categorical structure makes HD unusually actionable for practical use.

The core HD insight: trying to live against your design produces resistance and exhaustion, while living according to your design produces natural flow. The practice of HD is primarily about identifying how you are designed and then experimenting with making decisions and taking actions that align with that design rather than with generic self-help advice that assumes everyone is the same.

The 7 HD Authorities

Your authority is the decision-making mechanism built into your specific design. While your type tells you how energy flows for you, your authority tells you how to make decisions. Most modern HD teachers (following Pavaka Halelkatzir and others) consider authority more practically important than type.

Emotional Authority

Wait for the emotional wave to settle. ~50% of population.

Sacral Authority

Trust the sacral gut response. ~35% of population.

Splenic Authority

Trust the quiet spontaneous knowing. ~10% of population.

Ego Authority

Trust the willpower signal. Small population.

Self-Projected

Trust what comes through your own voice.

Mental Authority

No inner authority. External sounding-board decisions.

Lunar Authority

Reflector-specific. Wait a full lunar cycle.

The 12 HD Profiles

Your profile is a two-digit combination (1/3, 4/6, 5/1, etc.) drawn from the six lines of the I-Ching hexagram system. Each line has a specific archetypal quality, and the profile describes how you interact with the world and what life trajectory patterns are natural for you.

Essential HD Reading Path

If you are new to HD, here is the recommended learning path:

  1. Get your chart. Calculate your full chart first so you have specific placements to reference as you read.
  2. Understand your type. Read your type's specific guide. Start with the all-types overview if you are unsure which type you are.
  3. Find your authority. Read the authority overview, then drill into your specific authority's page.
  4. Understand your profile. Start with the profiles overview, then read your specific profile's deep dive.
  5. Learn your Definition. Read the Definition guide to understand whether you are Single, Split, Triple Split, Quadruple Split, or No Definition.
  6. Apply strategy. Begin experimenting with making decisions according to your type's strategy and your authority's mechanism. This is where HD becomes practical rather than theoretical.

Why HD Works Practically

Unlike some spiritual frameworks that require long study before practical application, HD becomes immediately usable once you know your type and authority. The core experiment HD asks of you is simple: make one decision per week according to your authority and see what happens. Over a few months of this experiment, most people report noticeably better outcomes than their previous default decision-making process.

This experimental quality distinguishes HD from purely descriptive frameworks. It also means the best way to evaluate HD is not by reading about it but by using it. Start with your chart, learn your authority, and run the experiment.
