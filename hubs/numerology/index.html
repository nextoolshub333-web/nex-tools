What Numerology Is

Numerology assigns meaning to numbers and to the numerical values derived from personal data (birth dates, names, significant dates). The core premise: numbers carry energetic signatures, and the numbers associated with a person reveal patterns about their personality, life direction, and soul lessons.

Unlike astrology, numerology requires minimal data to get started - just a birth date for Life Path calculation, or a birth name for Expression and Soul Urge. This accessibility has made numerology one of the most-searched spiritual topics globally. The simplicity is both its appeal and its limitation: numerology offers broad patterns rather than the granular detail of astrology or human design.

Master Numbers 11, 22, 33

Master numbers are double-digit numbers composed of repeating digits that are not reduced to a single digit during calculation. The three primary master numbers are 11 (the Intuitive), 22 (the Master Builder), and 33 (the Master Teacher).
