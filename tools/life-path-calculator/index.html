What is a Life Path Number?

The Life Path number is the foundation of your numerology chart. Calculated from your complete date of birth, it represents the core of who you are, your natural abilities, the challenges you will face, and the overall direction of your life journey.

Think of it as a blueprint for your life. While you always have free will, your Life Path number reveals the themes, lessons and opportunities that will consistently appear throughout your experience.

How is the Life Path Number Calculated?

The Life Path is calculated by reducing each component of your birth date (month, day, year) to a single digit, then adding those three digits together and reducing again. Master numbers (11, 22, 33) are preserved and not reduced further.

Example: Born March 15, 1990

  • Month: 3 (March = 3)
  • Day: 1+5 = 6
  • Year: 1+9+9+0 = 19 = 1+9 = 10 = 1+0 = 1
  • Life Path: 3+6+1 = 10 = 1+0 = 1

Life Path Number Compatibility

Life Path numbers reveal natural compatibility between people. Some numbers harmonize naturally, while others create dynamic tension that drives growth. Use both partners' Life Path numbers to understand your relationship dynamics.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the rarest Life Path number?

Life Path 33 is the rarest, followed by 22 and 11 (master numbers). Among single digits, Life Path 9 tends to be less common.

Can my Life Path number change?

No. Your Life Path number is derived from your birth date and remains constant throughout your life. However, your personal year number changes annually, bringing different energies each year.

What is the difference between Life Path and angel numbers?

Your Life Path number is permanent (based on your birthday) and describes your core personality and purpose. Angel numbers are repeating sequences you notice in daily life that carry timely spiritual messages.
