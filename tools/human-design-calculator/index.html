What is Human Design?

Human Design is a system of self-knowledge that combines astrology, the I Ching, Kabbalah, the Hindu-Brahmin chakra system and quantum physics into a single, unified framework. Created by Ra Uru Hu in 1987, it uses your exact birth date, time and location to generate a unique "BodyGraph" chart.

The 5 Human Design Types

Generator (about 37% of population)

Generators are the life force of the planet. They have sustainable, powerful energy for work they love. Their strategy is to wait to respond rather than initiating. When Generators follow their gut response (sacral authority), they find deep satisfaction.

Manifesting Generator (about 33%)

Manifesting Generators combine the initiating power of Manifestors with the sustained energy of Generators. They are fast, efficient multi-taskers who skip steps and find shortcuts. Strategy: wait to respond, then inform before acting.

Projector (about 20%)

Projectors are natural guides and managers who see others deeply. They do not have consistent energy for work, but excel at directing others' energy. Strategy: wait for the invitation. When recognized, Projectors experience success.

Manifestor (about 8%)

Manifestors are the initiators and trailblazers. They have the unique ability to start things and make an impact independently. Strategy: inform before acting. When Manifestors inform, they find peace.

Reflector (about 1%)

Reflectors are the rarest type with no defined centers. They mirror and amplify the energy around them. Strategy: wait a full lunar cycle (28 days) before making major decisions. Reflectors experience surprise and delight when aligned.

Human Design Authority Types

Your authority is how you are designed to make correct decisions:

  • Sacral - Gut response (Generators/MGs)
  • Emotional (Solar Plexus) - Wait for emotional clarity
  • Splenic - Instant intuitive knowing
  • Ego/Heart - Willpower and commitment
  • Self-Projected - Talk it out to hear your truth
  • Mental/Environmental - Discuss with trusted others
  • Lunar - Wait 28 days (Reflectors only)

Human Design Strategy

Your strategy is the way you are designed to interact with life. Following your strategy reduces resistance (what Human Design calls "not-self" behavior) and aligns you with your true nature. The key strategies are: respond (Generators), inform (Manifestors), wait for invitation (Projectors), and wait a lunar cycle (Reflectors).

FAQ

Do I need my exact birth time for Human Design?

For a precise BodyGraph chart, yes - even a few minutes can change your chart. This quiz gives you a likely type without needing birth time. For your full chart, visit Jovian Archive or MyBodyGraph.

Can my Human Design type change?

No. Your Human Design type is determined at birth and remains constant throughout your life. However, your understanding and embodiment of your type deepens over time.

What is the difference between Human Design and the Destiny Matrix?

Human Design requires exact birth time/location and maps your energy centers. The Destiny Matrix uses only your birthdate and works with 22 Arcana energies. They complement each other beautifully.
