What is the Soul Urge Number?

The Soul Urge number (also called the Heart's Desire number) is one of the five core numbers in your numerology chart. It reveals your deepest inner motivations - what you truly want at a soul level, often even when you are not consciously aware of it.

While your Life Path number shows your external journey, your Soul Urge reveals the internal fire that drives you. It answers the question: What does my heart truly long for?

How is the Soul Urge Calculated?

The Soul Urge uses only the vowels (A, E, I, O, U) from your full birth name. Each vowel has a numerological value:

  • A = 1, E = 5, I = 9, O = 6, U = 3

Add all the vowel values together and reduce to a single digit (or master number 11, 22, 33). The letter Y is treated as a vowel when it is the only vowel sound in a syllable (like in "Lynn" or "Bryce").

Soul Urge vs Life Path vs Expression

  • Soul Urge (vowels) - What you secretly desire
  • Life Path (birthday) - Your life's journey and purpose
  • Expression (all letters) - Your natural talents and abilities

Together, these three numbers paint a complete picture of who you are at a soul level.

FAQ

Should I use my married name or birth name?

Always use your full birth name (as on your birth certificate) for the Soul Urge calculation. Your birth name carries your original soul vibration.

What about the letter Y?

Y is counted as a vowel when it provides the vowel sound in a syllable (Bryce, Yvonne, Sally). Our calculator handles Y as a consonant by default. If Y is the primary vowel sound in your name, you may want to try both ways.
