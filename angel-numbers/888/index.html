What Does Angel Number 888 Mean?

Angel number 888 is the abundance flow. It appears when wealth, opportunity or material success is about to land in your life. The work is paying off. The integrity is being rewarded. The abundance is on its way. In Chinese culture, 8 is the most auspicious number because it sounds like the word for 'wealth' (fa). The Spiritual Meaning of 888

Spiritually, 888 confirms that you are in the receiving phase of a cycle. If you have been giving, serving, investing, or building, 888 marks the moment when the universe reciprocates. Ancient mystery schools taught that the infinity symbol (∞) turned sideways from 8 represents the eternal flow between giver and receiver, the cosmic law of reciprocation.
