Human Design Authority: All 7 Types Explained

TL;DR: Type tells you how your energy operates in the world. Are you a Generator who builds through response? A Projector who guides through invitation? A Manifestor who initiates? Type describes the pattern.

If you have read anything about Human Design, you know there are 5 types: Generator, Manifesting Generator, Projector, Manifestor, Reflector. Type gets most of the attention. But the real transformation happens at the authority level.

Your authority is the specific body signal you can trust for decisions. There are 7 authorities in Human Design, and following yours is the single most powerful practice in the entire system.

Why Authority Matters More Than Type

Type tells you how your energy operates in the world. Are you a Generator who builds through response? A Projector who guides through invitation? A Manifestor who initiates? Type describes the pattern.

Authority tells you how to make each individual decision inside that pattern. Should you take the job? Say yes to the date? Move cities? Start the business? Authority gives you a body-level yes or no.

When people say Human Design "changed their life," they usually mean this: they stopped deciding from their head and started deciding from their authority. That shift collapses years of trial-and-error into a few weeks of alignment.

The 7 Authorities

1. Emotional Authority (Solar Plexus) - About 50% of the Population

If your Solar Plexus center is defined, you have Emotional Authority. Your truth arrives over time, not in the moment. There is a wave: you feel high, you feel low, and somewhere between the peaks, your actual clarity lives.

The rule: never decide in the moment. Wait your wave. For small decisions, a few hours. For big decisions, days or weeks. The answer at the peak is not the truth. The answer at the low is not the truth. The answer in the quiet middle is.

Common mistake: saying yes at the high of the wave, then regretting it at the low.

2. Sacral Authority - Generators and Manifesting Generators

If your Sacral center is defined and your Solar Plexus is not, you have Sacral Authority. Your body responds in the moment with a sound or a lift: an energetic "uh-huh" (yes) or "uh-uh" (no). It is instant and pre-verbal.

The rule: have someone ask you yes/no questions. Notice the gut response before your mind speaks. Trust the first sound.

Common mistake: overriding a "no" because it seemed irrational, only to learn later your gut was right.

3. Splenic Authority - Some Manifestors and Projectors

If your Solar Plexus and Sacral are both undefined but your Spleen is defined, you have Splenic Authority. Your intuition speaks once, quietly, in the moment, and then it is gone. The spleen is the oldest awareness system in the body - survival, immunity, instinct.

The rule: act on the first whisper. If you hear "yes" or "no" or "now" or "wait," that is your truth. It does not repeat.

Common mistake: hearing the whisper, second-guessing it, and missing the window.

4. Ego Authority (Heart) - About 1% of the Population

Rare. Only some Manifestors and Projectors have this. Your decisions come from the Heart center - willpower and desire.

The rule: ask yourself "do I actually want this?" and "do I have the willpower to keep my word?" If both are yes, commit. If either is no, walk.

Common mistake: committing to things you do not truly want because you think you should. Ego authorities burn out from mismatched commitments faster than any other type.

5. Self-Projected Authority - Some Projectors

Projectors with undefined Solar Plexus, Sacral and Spleen, but defined G-Center, have Self-Projected Authority. Your truth comes out of your own mouth.

The rule: do not decide in silence. Talk it out - with a friend, a voice memo, even yourself in the car. Listen for what you say, not what the other person advises. The words that surprise you when they come out are your truth.

Common mistake: making decisions in your head and then wondering why they feel off.

6. Mental Authority (Environment) - Some Projectors

Projectors with no defined inner authority have Mental Authority. Your decisions depend on being in the right environment, around the right people. Your clarity is relational and spatial.

The rule: visit the place. Sit with the person. Sleep on it. Your body absorbs the environment and tells you through felt sense over time.

Common mistake: deciding from spreadsheets and pros/cons lists while sitting alone at home.

7. Lunar Authority - Reflectors Only (1% of the Population)

Reflectors have Lunar Authority, which means their clarity moves with the moon. Over a 28-day lunar cycle, your awareness cycles through every center in the chart.

The rule: for any big decision, wait a full lunar cycle (28 days). Talk to multiple trusted people. Let clarity emerge across the full cycle, not from a single moment.

Common mistake: letting others pressure you to decide faster than your 28-day rhythm.

Your mind has run your life for decades. It will not go quietly. Expect it to rationalize why your authority is wrong. Practice noticing the mental override without obeying it.

Common Misidentifications

Sacral Authority Mistaken for Emotional

Generators who have been told to "wait and feel things out" sometimes think they are emotional. If your Solar Plexus is undefined, your authority is Sacral. Trust the in-the-moment gut response.

Splenic Authority Mistaken for Gut (Sacral)

Splenic intuition feels similar to gut, but it is quieter and only speaks once. Sacral is louder and repeats. If the signal fades fast, it is splenic.

Self-Projected Mistaken for Mental

Both require talking it out. The difference: self-projected needs to hear their own voice. Mental needs to be in the right environment. Notice which one actually shifts your clarity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can my authority change over time?

No. Your authority is fixed at birth. What changes is your relationship to it. The more you practice listening, the clearer the signal becomes.

What if I am a Reflector with Lunar Authority and I need to decide fast?

For genuine emergencies, you can use your environment and trusted people to speed up the decision. But for anything that can wait 28 days, wait. Reflectors who honor their lunar rhythm report life-changing results within one cycle.

Is authority the same as intuition?

Authority is more specific. Intuition is a general word for knowing. Authority is the exact body mechanism your specific chart uses. Some people have intuition through the gut (Sacral), some through the spleen (Splenic), some through emotion over time (Emotional), and so on.

How long until I start trusting my authority?

Most people feel the first confirmation within 2-4 weeks of deliberate practice. Deep trust takes 3-6 months. By one year, authority feels like second nature.
