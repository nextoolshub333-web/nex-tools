Destiny Matrix Numbers 1-22: What Every Energy Means (Plus & Minus States)

Written by Nex Tools | April 16, 2026 | 18 min read

You pulled up your Destiny Matrix chart. You see numbers scattered across the diagram - along the lines, at the intersections, inside the squares. But what do those numbers actually mean?

Each number in your chart represents a specific energy. There are 22 energies in the Destiny Matrix system, each one linked to a Major Arcana card from the Tarot. But this is not fortune-telling. Think of it as a personality map - a blueprint that shows your natural strengths, your shadow patterns, and the lessons you are here to work through.

Every energy has two expressions:

  • Plus state - the healthy, balanced version. This is the energy working for you.
  • Minus state - the shadow, blocked version. This is the energy working against you.

The goal is not to eliminate your minus states. The goal is to recognize them so you can shift toward the plus. Awareness is the first step.

Below you will find all 22 energies with their core meaning, plus and minus expressions, career tendencies, and love patterns. Bookmark this page. You will come back to it.

Energy 1: The Magician

Archetype: The Magician

Plus State: People with Energy 1 in the plus are natural initiators. They have strong willpower, sharp communication skills, and the ability to turn ideas into reality. They pick up new skills fast and channel their focus into tangible results. They are the ones who start things - businesses, conversations, movements.

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 1 becomes manipulative. The gift of communication turns into deception or con-artistry. There can be scattered energy, starting dozens of projects and finishing none. Arrogance replaces confidence, and the person may use their charm to control others instead of creating something real.

Career: Entrepreneurship, sales, marketing, public speaking, coaching - any role that requires initiative and persuasion.

Love: In relationships, they need a partner who respects their independence and does not try to box them in.

Energy 2: The High Priestess

Archetype: The High Priestess

Plus State: Energy 2 in the plus gives deep intuition and emotional intelligence. These people sense what others feel before a word is spoken. They hold space for others naturally and have access to hidden knowledge - the kind that comes from stillness, not textbooks. They are gifted psychologists and healers.

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 2 creates secrecy and emotional manipulation. The person may withhold information as a power play or become overly passive, waiting for someone else to make decisions. Paranoia and jealousy can take root when they stop trusting their own inner voice and start projecting fears onto others.

Career: Psychology, counseling, research, occult studies, writing, or any field that values depth over speed.

Love: They need emotional transparency and a partner who values deep conversation over surface-level interaction.

Energy 3: The Empress

Archetype: The Empress

Plus State: Energy 3 radiates abundance and creative flow. These people attract beauty, comfort, and resources naturally. They nurture others without depleting themselves and often have a strong connection to nature, art, or the body. Everything they touch tends to grow - gardens, businesses, families, friendships.

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 3 flips into materialism, laziness, or smothering behavior. The nurturing instinct becomes controlling. There can be overindulgence in food, shopping, or sensory pleasures as a way to fill an emotional void. Some people with this energy in the minus neglect themselves entirely while pouring everything into others.

Career: Design, fashion, agriculture, hospitality, childcare, beauty industry, or creative arts.

Love: They thrive with a partner who appreciates their warmth but does not take their generosity for granted.

Energy 4: The Emperor

Archetype: The Emperor

Plus State: Energy 4 in the plus brings structure, discipline, and grounded leadership. These people build systems that last. They take responsibility seriously and create stability for everyone around them. They are the ones who turn chaos into order - reliable, strategic, and protective of those in their care.

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 4 becomes a tyrant. Rigidity replaces structure. The need for control extends into micromanagement, emotional coldness, or authoritarian behavior. Some people with this energy suppress their emotions entirely, believing that vulnerability equals weakness. Others become workaholics who sacrifice relationships for status.

Career: Management, government, law, military, construction, finance - any role that demands authority and long-term planning.

Love: They need a partner who is strong enough to challenge them without triggering their need to dominate.

Energy 5: The Hierophant

Archetype: The Hierophant

Plus State: Energy 5 in the plus produces natural teachers and mentors. These people carry wisdom that feels ancient - they understand traditions, systems of knowledge, and how to transmit complex ideas in simple ways. They bridge the gap between the spiritual and the practical. Others seek them out for guidance instinctively.

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 5 becomes dogmatic and rigid. The teacher turns into a preacher who insists their way is the only way. There can be an obsession with rules, religious fanaticism, or moral superiority. Some people with this energy in the minus reject all structure entirely, swinging to the opposite extreme of rebellion without purpose.

Career: Education, religion, philosophy, law, mentoring, consulting, or spiritual guidance.

Love: They need a partner who shares their values and respects their need for meaningful conversation over small talk.

Energy 6: The Lovers

Archetype: The Lovers

Plus State: Energy 6 in the plus is about authentic choice and deep connection. These people understand that real love requires choosing - every day, deliberately. They harmonize opposites naturally and have a gift for bringing people together. Their relationships feel balanced because they give and receive equally.

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 6 creates indecisiveness and codependency. The person cannot choose - between partners, paths, or priorities. They may stay in toxic relationships out of fear of being alone or swing between extremes without ever committing. Jealousy, love triangles, and emotional paralysis are common patterns.

Career: Relationship coaching, diplomacy, mediation, art, fashion, matchmaking, or team leadership.

Love: They must learn to choose themselves first before they can build a healthy partnership with someone else.

Energy 7: The Chariot

Archetype: The Chariot

Plus State: Energy 7 in the plus is pure drive. These people set a goal and move toward it with focused determination. They overcome obstacles that would stop most people. They are competitive in a healthy way - pushing themselves to grow rather than tearing others down. Travel, movement, and new experiences fuel them.

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 7 becomes aggressive or reckless. The drive turns into obsession. These people may bulldoze over others to get what they want, ignore their health in pursuit of a goal, or become addicted to the rush of winning. Some crash hard after achieving a target because they never learned to enjoy the process.

Career: Sports, transportation, logistics, automotive, military, competitive business, or anything involving speed and strategy.

Love: They need a partner who can match their pace and does not try to slow them down or clip their wings.

Energy 8: Strength (Justice)

Archetype: Strength

Plus State: Energy 8 in the plus combines courage with compassion. These people have immense inner power but express it gently. They do not need to shout to be heard. Their strength comes from patience, self-mastery, and the ability to stay calm when everyone else is panicking. They tame their own inner beasts before trying to change the world.

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 8 produces either explosive rage or complete suppression. The person may swing between being overly aggressive and being a doormat. Fear of their own power leads to self-sabotage. Some people with this energy attract conflict repeatedly because they have not learned to channel their intensity constructively.

Career: Law, advocacy, veterinary work, physical therapy, martial arts, psychology, or social justice.

Love: They need a partner who is not intimidated by their intensity and who can hold space for both their strength and vulnerability.

Energy 9: The Hermit

Archetype: The Hermit

Plus State: Energy 9 in the plus is the wise sage who has done the inner work. These people have deep self-knowledge gained through solitude and reflection. They carry a quiet light that others are drawn to. They do not need external validation because their sense of worth comes from within. They make excellent guides for those who are lost.

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 9 becomes isolation and bitterness. The person withdraws not for growth but out of fear. They may feel superior to others and use their intelligence as a wall. Loneliness, depression, and a refusal to ask for help are common patterns. Some become so detached from the world that they lose the ability to connect meaningfully.

Career: Research, academia, spiritual teaching, writing, technology, science, or any field that rewards independent thinking.

Love: They need a partner who respects their need for alone time without interpreting it as rejection.

Energy 10: Wheel of Fortune

Archetype: Wheel of Fortune

Plus State: Energy 10 in the plus brings natural luck and an understanding of cycles. These people flow with change instead of fighting it. They sense when a door is opening and walk through it without overthinking. Money and opportunities tend to find them because they trust the process. They understand that ups and downs are part of the same wheel.

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 10 creates gambling addiction, financial instability, and a victim mentality. The person blames fate for their problems instead of taking action. They may ride highs recklessly and crash into lows without learning the lesson. Compulsive risk-taking or extreme passivity - both are shadow expressions of this energy.

Career: Finance, investing, entertainment, travel industry, events, or anything involving timing and calculated risk.

Love: They need a partner who can handle uncertainty and does not demand a perfectly predictable life.

Energy 11: Justice (Strength)

Archetype: Justice

Plus State: Energy 11 in the plus is about radical fairness. These people have an innate sense of right and wrong that goes beyond social norms. They fight for balance - in their own lives and in the systems around them. They take responsibility for their actions and understand that every cause has an effect. Their word is their bond.

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 11 becomes judgmental and punishing. The person appoints themselves as judge over everyone and cannot forgive - not others, not themselves. They may obsess over fairness to the point of paralysis, or they swing the other way into corruption and hypocrisy. Karmic debt feels heavy for this energy when it is blocked.

Career: Law, judiciary, human rights, compliance, auditing, ethics, mediation, or activism.

Love: They need a partner who shares their sense of integrity and is willing to have honest, sometimes uncomfortable conversations.

Energy 12: The Hanged Man

Archetype: The Hanged Man

Plus State: Energy 12 in the plus grants a rare gift: the ability to see things from a completely different angle. These people surrender control willingly and gain wisdom from it. They understand that sometimes doing nothing is the most powerful action. They sacrifice personal comfort for a greater purpose and find meaning in the waiting.

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 12 creates a martyr complex. The person sacrifices themselves endlessly but keeps score. They feel stuck, unable to move forward, and blame everyone else for their paralysis. There can be addiction, escapism, or a pattern of playing the victim. They give up their power and then resent others for taking it.

Career: Non-profit work, healthcare, spiritual service, art, film, photography, or roles that require seeing the world differently.

Love: They need a partner who does not exploit their willingness to sacrifice and who encourages them to set boundaries.

Energy 13: Transformation

Archetype: Death / Transformation

Plus State: Energy 13 in the plus is about radical renewal. These people are not afraid of endings because they understand that every death creates space for new life. They reinvent themselves multiple times and help others navigate major transitions. They have a deep relationship with truth - the kind that strips away what is fake and leaves only what is real.

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 13 manifests as a fear of change so intense it creates stagnation. The person clings to dead relationships, dead jobs, dead patterns. Or they swing to the other extreme and destroy things compulsively - burning bridges, pushing people away, creating chaos because stillness feels threatening. Obsession with death or morbid thinking can surface.

Career: Surgery, crisis management, funeral services, psychology, debt restructuring, renovation, or any field that involves dismantling and rebuilding.

Love: They need a partner who embraces transformation and does not try to keep things the way they were.

Energy 14: Temperance

Archetype: Temperance

Plus State: Energy 14 in the plus is the master of balance. These people blend opposites - logic and emotion, work and play, giving and receiving. They have a natural talent for moderation and know how to take the best parts of two things and create something better. They are patient, methodical, and deeply grounded. Healing often comes naturally to them.

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 14 creates extremes. The person swings between overworking and total collapse, binge eating and starvation, obsessive control and complete chaos. They cannot find the middle ground. Impatience and frustration build up because they know what balance looks like but cannot achieve it. Addictive behaviors may emerge as failed attempts at self-regulation.

Career: Medicine, pharmacology, chemistry, cooking, winemaking, holistic health, or conflict resolution.

Love: They need a partner who is emotionally stable and does not pull them into extremes.

Energy 15: The Devil

Archetype: The Devil

Plus State: Energy 15 in the plus is raw magnetism and material power. These people understand money, desire, and the physical world at a deep level. They can build wealth, attract attention, and create tangible results faster than most. Their shadow is not hidden - they have already looked at it, accepted it, and integrated it. That honesty makes them powerful.

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 15 is the classic addiction pattern. Drugs, sex, gambling, power, shopping - any substance or behavior that creates dependency. The person becomes a slave to their desires instead of mastering them. Manipulation, narcissism, and toxic relationships are common. They may also project their shadow onto others, seeing evil everywhere except in the mirror.

Career: Finance, real estate, entertainment, nightlife, sexuality research, addiction counseling (once healed), or entrepreneurship.

Love: They need a partner who sees through their surface charm and loves the real person underneath - not the mask.

Energy 16: The Tower

Archetype: The Tower

Plus State: Energy 16 in the plus brings revolutionary clarity. These people are the ones who tear down what is broken so something better can be built. They speak uncomfortable truths. They innovate, disrupt, and refuse to maintain systems that do not work. After every collapse in their life, they come back stronger - and they know it.

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 16 attracts sudden catastrophe. Job losses, breakups, health crises, financial crashes - they seem to come out of nowhere. But they are not random. They happen because the person has been ignoring warning signs for too long. There can be a destructive streak - picking fights, sabotaging good things, or living in constant anxiety about the next disaster.

Career: Architecture, demolition, crisis response, innovation, tech disruption, emergency medicine, or political reform.

Love: They need a partner who can handle intensity and does not run when life gets turbulent.

Energy 17: The Star

Archetype: The Star

Plus State: Energy 17 in the plus radiates hope and inspiration. These people are natural healers who restore faith in others just by being present. They have a strong connection to something larger - call it the universe, God, source, or simply a deep inner knowing that everything will be okay. Their creativity flows effortlessly, and they often have a talent for music, art, or writing that touches people deeply.

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 17 creates disillusionment and despair. The person loses their connection to hope and becomes cynical. They may chase fame or recognition to fill a spiritual void. Unrealistic expectations lead to constant disappointment. Some people with this energy in shadow become emotionally cold - they shut down their sensitivity because the world feels too harsh.

Career: Music, art, astrology, spiritual healing, environmental work, humanitarian causes, or therapy.

Love: They need a partner who nurtures their dreams without calling them naive or unrealistic.

Energy 18: The Moon

Archetype: The Moon

Plus State: Energy 18 in the plus gives access to the deepest layers of the unconscious. These people have vivid dreams, powerful intuition, and an ability to sense things that others miss entirely. They navigate uncertainty with grace because they trust what they feel even when they cannot explain it. Creative work that emerges from this energy often has a haunting, otherworldly quality.

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 18 becomes a fog of illusion and anxiety. The person cannot distinguish between real danger and imagined threats. Phobias, nightmares, paranoia, and deception dominate. They may lie to themselves and others as a coping mechanism. The imagination that could create art instead creates worst-case scenarios on repeat.

Career: Psychology, dream analysis, film, fiction writing, marine biology, night-shift work, or investigative roles.

Love: They need a partner who provides emotional safety without dismissing their inner world as irrational.

Energy 19: The Sun

Archetype: The Sun

Plus State: Energy 19 in the plus is pure vitality and joy. These people light up a room. They have natural charisma, genuine optimism, and the ability to make others feel good about themselves. Success comes to them because they radiate confidence without arrogance. They lead by example, inspire through action, and maintain their inner child no matter how old they get.

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 19 becomes ego inflation or burnout. The person needs constant attention, praise, and center stage. When the spotlight fades, depression hits hard. Some become performative - faking happiness while falling apart inside. Others burn themselves out trying to be everything to everyone. The joy becomes a mask instead of a genuine state.

Career: Performing arts, leadership, childcare, event planning, motivational speaking, or any role where their presence lifts the entire team.

Love: They need a partner who genuinely celebrates them without competing for attention or feeding their ego.

Energy 20: Judgement

Archetype: Judgement

Plus State: Energy 20 in the plus is about answering the call. These people feel a deep sense of purpose - like they were born to do something specific. When they align with that calling, everything clicks. They have the ability to wake others up, to help people see their own potential, and to create movements that outlast them. They live by a personal code that transcends social expectations.

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 20 creates a paralyzing fear of judgment - from others, from God, from themselves. The person knows they are meant for more but cannot take the leap. They may spend their entire life in a safe, comfortable role that slowly suffocates them. Guilt, regret, and the haunting feeling of wasted potential are the hallmarks of this energy in shadow.

Career: Motivational speaking, music, spiritual leadership, advocacy, therapy, social entrepreneurship, or any purpose-driven work.

Love: They need a partner who supports their mission and does not ask them to shrink for the sake of comfort.

Energy 21: The World

Archetype: The World

Plus State: Energy 21 in the plus is the energy of completion and mastery. These people see the big picture. They integrate all the lessons, connect all the dots, and create things that feel whole and finished. They travel well - both physically and intellectually - and they understand that every ending is also a beginning. Their life feels like a full circle, not a straight line.

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 21 creates a feeling of being trapped or incomplete. The person feels like something is always missing, no matter how much they achieve. They may start a hundred projects and finish none, or they cycle through relationships without ever feeling satisfied. A restlessness that nothing can cure - that is Energy 21 in shadow.

Career: International business, travel, publishing, diplomacy, large-scale project management, or any field with a global scope.

Love: They need a partner who shares their love for growth and exploration and does not anchor them to one narrow version of life.

Energy 22: The Fool

Archetype: The Fool

Plus State: Energy 22 in the plus is fearless innocence. These people leap before they look - and somehow land on their feet. They have a childlike curiosity that keeps them open to experiences most adults have closed off. They trust life at a fundamental level. Innovation, creativity, and unexpected breakthroughs come naturally because they are not weighed down by "how things should be."

Minus State: In the minus, Energy 22 becomes recklessness and irresponsibility. The person makes impulsive decisions with real consequences - financial, relational, physical. They refuse to grow up, dodge accountability, and repeat the same mistakes because they never stop long enough to learn. Naivety is charming at 20. At 40, it becomes a liability.

Career: Startups, comedy, travel blogging, extreme sports, innovation labs, or any role where unconventional thinking is rewarded.

Love: They need a partner who embraces spontaneity but also provides enough grounding to keep the relationship stable.

How to Use This Guide

Knowing the 22 energies is step one. Here is how to actually apply them:

  1. Calculate your chart first. Use a Destiny Matrix calculator to get your personal numbers. Without your chart, this guide is just theory.
  2. Start with your center number. This is your core energy - the one that defines your fundamental nature. Read its plus and minus states carefully and honestly assess where you stand.
  3. Look at your karmic tail. These are the energies you are meant to work through in this lifetime. They will feel like your biggest challenges - and your greatest growth opportunities.
  4. Check your comfort zone. These energies come naturally to you. They are your strengths, but they can also become crutches if you hide in them instead of growing.
  5. Track your shifts. Over weeks and months, notice when you slip into minus states. Awareness alone starts the shift toward plus. You do not need to force anything.

The Destiny Matrix is not a life sentence. It is a map. And like any map, it only helps if you use it to move.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I have the same energy appear multiple times in my chart?

Yes. When an energy appears in multiple positions, it means that theme is amplified in your life. You will experience both its plus and minus states more intensely than someone who has it in only one position.

What if most of my energies are in the minus state?

That is not a permanent condition. It simply means you have more room for growth. Most people operate in a mix of plus and minus across different areas. The minus states usually point to lessons from childhood or past patterns that need conscious attention.

Are some energies better than others?

No. Every energy has equal potential for greatness and struggle. Energy 19 (The Sun) sounds positive, but in the minus it creates ego problems and burnout. Energy 15 (The Devil) sounds scary, but in the plus it creates extraordinary material success and self-awareness. The quality of your life depends on the state of your energies, not the numbers themselves.

How is Destiny Matrix different from regular Tarot?

Tarot readings change depending on the question, the reader, and the spread. Your Destiny Matrix chart is fixed - it is based on your birth date and does not change. Think of Tarot as a conversation and Destiny Matrix as a permanent blueprint.
