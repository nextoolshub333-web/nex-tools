What Is Human Design Authority?

Lunar Authority operates through the 28-day lunar cycle - the time it takes for the moon to move through all 12 zodiac signs and touch every gate in the Human Design chart. Reflectors, who have no defined centers, use this cyclical movement as their decision-making framework. By cycle's end, a clarity has accumulated that no single moment could provide. How to Use Lunar Authority (Reflector) for Decisions

Using Lunar Authority requires respecting the 28-day cycle for major decisions. The practice: when a significant decision arises, mark the date and commit to no decision for 28 days. During that time, talk about the decision with trusted advisors, visit relevant environments, sleep in different places if possible, notice how your clarity shifts across different moon phases. At day 28, you will know. For smaller decisions, you can use shorter timeframes - but the general principle is that Reflector clarity accumulates rather than arriving suddenly. Partners, employers, and family of Reflectors must understand this pace, or they will constantly pressure the Reflector into wrong decisions.

The Not-Self Pattern if You Ignore Lunar Authority (Reflector)

The not-self pattern of Lunar Authority is living on everyone else's timing rather than the moon's. The unhealthy Reflector tries to keep up with faster authorities - Sacral, Splenic, Ego - and consistently makes wrong decisions because the cycle was not complete. They often feel chronically exhausted because they are reading everyone else's energy without boundary. The healing: radical commitment to the 28-day cycle for major decisions, plus energy practices to protect their sensitivity (solitude, nature time, limiting crowds). Reflectors who honor their design become the wisest people in any room - they see what everyone misses.
